1. Introduction

At ImgAURA, we take your privacy critically. Our application is built on a client-side architecture, meaning that all image processing happens directly in your browser using WebAssembly, WebGL, and JavaScript.

🔐 Key Promise: Your images are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers. What happens in your browser, stays in your browser.

2. How We Process Your Data

When you use our tools (Background Remover, Image Enhancer, Object Detector, etc.), the AI models run entirely on your device:

  • TensorFlow.js and Google MediaPipe run locally in your browser
  • Images are processed using your device's CPU/GPU via WebGL
  • Processed results are generated in-memory and never sent to any server
  • When you download a result, it's saved directly from your browser to your device

We have zero access to your original images, processed results, or any data you work with.

3. Usage Analytics

We use minimal, anonymized local storage to improve user experience:

  • Local counters track which tools you use (stored only in your browser)
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected
  • No IP addresses, locations, or device fingerprints are logged
  • This data never leaves your device and is used only for internal feature improvements

You can clear this data anytime by clearing your browser's local storage.

4. Third-Party Services

ImgAURA relies on trusted Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load essential libraries:

  • TensorFlow.js (cdn.jsdelivr.net) - AI/ML library
  • Google MediaPipe (cdn.jsdelivr.net) - Computer vision models
  • Tailwind CSS (cdn.tailwindcss.com) - Styling framework
  • Adsterra (pl28909645.effectivegatecpm.com) - Advertising network (see Section 6)

These providers may log standard connection data (IP, user agent) per their own privacy policies. We do not control their practices but choose reputable partners.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

ImgAURA uses minimal browser storage:

  • Local Storage: Stores anonymous tool usage counters (e.g., "bg-remover-used: 5")
  • Session Storage: Temporarily holds UI preferences during your visit
  • No tracking cookies: We do not use third-party tracking cookies or pixels

You can manage or delete this data through your browser's settings at any time.

6. Advertising & Monetization

ImgAURA is free forever. To sustain development, we display non-intrusive ads:

  • Ads are served by Adsterra and similar networks
  • Ad providers may use cookies or device identifiers for ad personalization
  • Ad clicks may be tracked for performance analytics by the ad network
  • Your images are never shared with ad networks for targeting purposes

You can use ad-blockers if preferred, though we appreciate your support in keeping ImgAURA free.

7. Children's Privacy

ImgAURA is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Your Rights

Because we don't collect or store your personal data, you inherently have full control:

  • Right to Access: All your data stays on your device
  • Right to Delete: Clear browser storage anytime via browser settings
  • Right to Portability: Download your processed images anytime
  • Right to Object: Use an ad-blocker or stop using the service anytime

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated revision date
  • Highlighted with a summary of material changes
  • Effective immediately upon posting

By continuing to use ImgAURA after changes, you agree to the revised policy.
