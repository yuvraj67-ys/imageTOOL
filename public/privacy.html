Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At ImgAURA, we take your privacy critically. Our application is built on a client-side architecture, meaning that all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers.

2. How We Process Your Data

When you use our tools (like Background Remover, Image Enhancer, etc.), the AI models run entirely on your device using WebGL and WebAssembly. We do not have access to your original or processed images.

3. Usage Analytics

We use simple, anonymized local storage counters to track which tools you use most frequently, solely to improve the user experience. This data never leaves your device and contains no personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on carefully selected Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load necessary libraries (such as TensorFlow.js, MediaPipe, and Tailwind CSS). These providers have their own privacy policies regarding connection logs.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. By continuing to use ImgAURA, you agree to the revised policy.
